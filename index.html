With two consecutive 2026 rate hikes covered, launch a recurring "Rate Watch" carousel each RBA decision week to build habitual engagement and position Lendi as the go-to mortgage rate authority.

All 3 active posts were published on March 30 within 100 minutes (5:08am–6:44am UTC / 4–5pm AEDT), cannibalising each other's algorithm distribution budget. Growth
Reels Are the Missing Discovery Lever
With 3 followers and 8 accounts reached, nearly all reach is already non-follower — strong for a new account. But all 3 posts are feed-only. Instagram surfaces Reels 3–5x more frequently in Explore and Suggested feeds. A single 30-second "What the RBA cash rate at 4.10% means for your mortgage" Reel could triple weekly reach. Target 1 Reel per week to activate the discovery algorithm.

Strategy

Every post includes a strong direct-response CTA ("Link in bio", "DM us RATE", "DM us HOME"). The conversion path is built.
